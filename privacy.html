Privacy Policy

Effective: 01/06/2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Parent/guardian names, contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Student names, birth years (not full dates), school level, and academic needs
  • Payment details (processed through secure third-party providers like FPS)
  • Session notes and progress records (stored anonymously when possible)
  • Limited health/special needs info if relevant to tutoring (e.g., dyslexia)

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is only used to:

  • Provide and personalize tutoring services
  • Communicate about sessions, invoices, and progress reports
  • Ensure student safety during in-person sessions

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures including:

  • Deletion of payment data after 1 year.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Payment processors (FPS) for transactions
  • Educational apps with parent consent
  • Hong Kong authorities if legally required (e.g., court order)

5. Parental Rights

Under Hong Kong law, parents may:

  • Request access to or correction of their child's records within 30 days
  • Withdraw consent (may affect service continuity)
  • Request anonymization of historical session notes

6. Children's Data

Additional protections for students:

  • No personal data collected beyond what's necessary for tutoring
  • No social media integration or online profiles
  • No photos/videos or only with signed parental consent for the parents/ guardians own use
