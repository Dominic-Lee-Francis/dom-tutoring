Privacy Policy

Effective: 01/06/2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Parent's/Guardian's name and contact details (email, phone, address).
  • Student's name, birth year (not the full date), school level, and academic needs.
  • Payment details (processed through secure third-party providers, such as FPS).
  • Session notes and progress records (stored anonymously when possible).
  • Limited health/special needs information if relevant to enhancing the tutoring service (e.g., dyslexia).

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is only used to:

  • Provide and personalise tutoring services.
  • Communicate on sessions, invoices, and progress reports.
  • Ensure student safety during in-person sessions.

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures including:

  • Deletion of payment data after 1 year.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Payment processors (such as FPS) for transactions.
  • Educational apps with parent consent.
  • Hong Kong authorities if legally required (e.g., court order).

5. Children's Data

Additional protections for students:

  • No personal data collected beyond what's necessary for tutoring.
  • No social media integration or online profiles.
  • No photos or videos will be taken of children. Photos will be taken only with signed parental consent for the parent's/guardian's own use.
