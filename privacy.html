Privacy Policy

Effective: 01/06/2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Parent/guardian names and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Student names, ages, and academic information
  • Payment details (processed through secure third-party providers)
  • Communication records (emails, messages, feedback)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and personalize our tutoring services
  • Communicate with parents about sessions and progress
  • Process payments and manage accounts
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations in Hong Kong

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Secure storage of physical and digital records
  • Password protection and encryption where appropriate
  • Limited access to personal information

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors for transaction completion
  • Educational authorities if required by Hong Kong law
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

5. Parental Rights

Parents/guardians may:

  • Request access to their child's information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (may affect services)
  • Request deletion of data where legally permissible

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on our website with a new effective date.
